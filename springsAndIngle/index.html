Springs & Fire

Water + Fire. Springs and Fire. A documentation of the amazing places we collect water and cook meals. When we use a fire ring we clean it, remove all the old ash to make room for the new, and more importantly since we cook break directly on the coals to get out any garbage we don't want to burn into our food. We bring a grill, generally, and leave it built into the fire for future wanderers to enjoy. Fire is essential to us, as humans, let's not forget that and enjoy the mesmerizing ritual of living by fire, of feeling warm and safe by it's glow, and eating good hot food.

Fire Ritual

  • Clean Fire Pit
  • Arrange three rocks to support the pots
  • Make grill support
  • Start Fire, Make Coals
  • Boil Water
  • Start Dall
  • Make Dough
  • Drink Hot Water
  • Start Cooking Roti
  • Eat
  • Drink More Hot Water
  • Clean Up
  • Stare at Fire
